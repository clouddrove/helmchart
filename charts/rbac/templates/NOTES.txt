This chart creates, per member:
- Namespace (optional)
- ServiceAccount: sa-<name>
- Secret (token): sa-<name>-token
- RoleBinding to ClusterRole (<role>) within the namespace

List created SAs:
  kubectl get sa -A -l rbac-homelab=member -o wide

List token secrets:
  kubectl get secret -A -l rbac-homelab=member
